Research Page

Welcome to the research page of Nepali chitra printing.Here we have added the websites and concepts that we used as references to complete our fully functional website. This page also includes comparisons, differences, parallels, and innovations between our website and other existing websites. The primary goal of this page is to do a comparative examination of our website with other current existing websites.

Website comparison

Home page

Similarities

The image on the left represents our home page, while the one on the left shows an existing website named Daraz, from which we drew inspiration when designing our home page. As a reference for our website's home page, we can see some parallels between both, such as the concept of an image slider that displays several interesting deals currently accessible while sliding. The locations of the logo and navigation bar are fairly identical on both websites.

Differences

Despite their similarities, there are some notable differences between the websites, such as color and theme. We can also see that our website does not include a search bar in the navigation bar like the other website. Our website includes a short description about our website and web pages with tansition image that other website lackks.

Innovations

Though being a reference website it sure does have some features that our website lacks but our website also has some features that it lacks such as the ability to switch between light and dark modes. Our website also includes brief descriptions of each page, as well as links to those pages. We also have image transitions on our website.

Product page

Similarities

The image on the left reflects our website's product page, whereas the image on the left represents an existing product page from a website called Daraz, which we used as inspiration when building our product page. We can see certain similarities between them, such as how each product is shown in a box with an image of the product. Both websites display a star rating, which indicates the product's rating. We can also see that the discounted price is represented in the same way on both websites.

Differences

Although having certain similarities, there are some differences between the websites like our website lacks the feature of search bar near nevigation bar. The reference website also has the feature of filttering products according to brand price and rating which our website lacks. The color and theme between the 2 website is also very much different from each other. Despite the fact that both websites serve as online purchasing platforms, ours solely sells printing machines.

Innovations

Although Daraz is a reference website, it does have features that stand out more than ours, but our website also has a few features that stand out more than the reference website. Our website includes a function for switching between light and dark modes. Our website also does a better job showing star ratings with actual star symbols. Our website also performs better at delivering minimal product data from the see more option, making it easier for clients to view printer characteristics while scrolling across pages. Also, our website makes it easier to view product warranties.
